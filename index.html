I'm building an AI tool for neurodivergent professionals to catch the unspoken cues during conversations you might miss — body language shifts, tone changes, contradictions between what someone says and how they actually seem. Your honest feedback shapes what this becomes.

Thanks, Seth Stapleton — Founder, Nerovion

How It Works

During a video call, Nerovion analyzes facial expressions, tone, and body language in real time — then flags what you might be missing.

"They said 'sounds good' but their tone sounds hesitant and they looked away. They might have concerns they haven't voiced."
